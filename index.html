Community Engagement Program

A supportive weekday program where adults with disabilities build friendships, explore new interests, and connect with their community.

What to Expect

IMAGE #2

Daily activities - Participants engaging together

At Options For All, every day is an opportunity to connect, grow, and thrive. Our Community Engagement Program is 100% community-based and provides a welcoming space where adults with disabilities can build meaningful friendships, discover new passions, and participate actively in their local community.

We maintain a 3:1 participant-to-staff ratio to ensure consistent support and individualized attention. We meet Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Door-to-door transportation is included. We'll pick you up in the morning and bring you home in the afternoon. Routes are personalized based on where you live.

Every person brings unique strengths and interests. That's why we create individualized daily plans that honor what matters most to each participant. Whether you want to try something brand new or dive deeper into a favorite activity, our caring team is here to support you every step of the way.

For over 40 years, Options For All has been a trusted partner for families across San Diego. Our experienced, compassionate staff create an environment where everyone belongs, every voice is heard, and every day brings new possibilities.

Is This Program Right for You?

Our Community Engagement Program welcomes adults with disabilities who are:

Seeking Connection

Ready to build friendships and feel part of a supportive community

Enjoying Structure

Thriving with a consistent weekday routine and organized activities

Exploring Interests

Excited to try new things and develop skills in a fun, low-pressure setting

Staying Active

Wanting to get out, stay engaged, and participate in their local community

Not sure if it's the right fit? We're here to talk through your questions and help you explore what works best for you or your loved one.

About Options For All

Options For All is a nonprofit organization with more than 40 years of experience supporting individuals and families through high-quality, community-based programs.

We proudly partner with the San Diego Regional Center and the Department of Rehabilitation to help individuals access services that support meaningful community connection and long-term success.

We are committed to creating inclusive, welcoming spaces where people feel valued, supported, and connected. Our programs are built around consistency, relationship-building, and person-centered support, allowing participants to grow at their own pace while staying actively engaged in their communities.

Our long-standing presence in the community reflects our focus on quality, trust, and meaningful outcomes. Families choose Options For All because of our experienced staff, thoughtful programming, and deep commitment to belonging and inclusion.
