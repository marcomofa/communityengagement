About the Program

The Community Engagement Program at Options For All is a supportive day program where participants can build friendships, explore interests, and take part in meaningful activities within their community.

The program runs Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM and includes transportation for morning pick-up and afternoon return. Transportation schedules are coordinated based on participant location.

Each day is thoughtfully planned to reflect individual interests and preferences. Participants are encouraged to try new activities, build confidence, and stay engaged in a welcoming, structured environment.

With more than 40 years of experience, Options For All is known for inclusive programs led by caring, well-trained staff who focus on connection, respect, and belonging.

Is This a Good Fit?

This program may be a great match for individuals who want a weekday routine, enjoy small group activities, and are excited to be active in their community.

You might love this program if you:

  • Want a supportive weekday program with structure
  • Enjoy social connection and participating with others
  • Like trying new activities and building everyday skills
  • Want to stay engaged in your local community
